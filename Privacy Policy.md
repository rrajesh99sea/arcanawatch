# Privacy Policy for Arcana

**Last updated: June 14, 2026**

Arcana ("the App") is a watch face app for Apple Watch developed by Rajesh Ramanathan ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

## Summary

Arcana does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All data accessed by the App is used solely to display information on your watch face (such as the time, your heart rate, activity progress, and local weather) and remains on your device. We have no servers, no analytics, and no third-party data sharing.

## Information the App Accesses

### Health Data (HealthKit)

With your permission, Arcana reads your current heart rate and activity ring progress (move, exercise, and stand data) from HealthKit. This data is displayed directly on the watch face and is:

- Never stored by the App beyond what is needed to render the current display
- Never transmitted off your device
- Never shared with us, advertisers, or any third party

You can revoke this access at any time in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Arcana** on your iPhone.

### Location Data

With your permission, Arcana uses your device's location (only while the App is in use) to determine local weather conditions for display on certain watch faces. Location data is:

- Used only to retrieve weather information
- Never stored, logged, or transmitted to us
- Never shared with any third party other than Apple's WeatherKit service (see below)

You can revoke this access at any time in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Arcana** on your iPhone.

### Weather Data (Apple WeatherKit)

Arcana uses Apple's WeatherKit service to retrieve current weather conditions for your approximate location. This request is made directly between your device and Apple, governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We do not receive or have access to this data.

## Data We Do Not Collect

Arcana does not collect:

- Your name, email address, or any contact information
- Analytics, usage statistics, or crash reports
- Advertising identifiers
- Any data that identifies you personally

## Children's Privacy

Arcana does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App does not collect personal information from any user, regardless of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
