## Description

Arcana brings a collection of meticulously detailed analog watch faces to your Apple Watch — each inspired by a classic timepiece style, rebuilt from the ground up for your wrist.

**Six faces, six personalities**

- **Airfield** — A Type B pilot's watch with a bold 24-hour outer scale, high-contrast dial, and lume-filled hands built for legibility at a glance.
- **Chronicler** — A classic three-subdial chronograph layout that keeps time, date, and activity organized in one elegant view.
- **Depths** — A dive-watch face with a rotating-style bezel ring, bright lume accents, and rugged good looks.
- **Meridian** — A refined dual-register dress face for everyday wear.
- **DualTime** — A four-up world-time face showing the time in cities around the globe — tap any quadrant to cycle through more than a dozen time zones.
- **SWS Solar Carbon** — A tactical carbon-fiber dial with bold markers, skeletonized paddle hands, and a signature red chronograph seconds hand.

**Smart complications, built in**

- Live heart rate from Apple Health
- Daily activity ring progress (move, exercise, stand)
- Local weather conditions via Apple WeatherKit
- Smooth, continuously sweeping seconds hands

**Designed for Apple Watch**

Arcana is a standalone watch app — no iPhone companion required. Switch between faces with a simple swipe and make any of them your watch face from the Watch Face gallery.

All health and location data stays on your device — Arcana never collects, stores, or transmits your personal information.
